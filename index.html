About Us

Empowering Youth with Technical Skills

The St. Theresa’s Industrial Training Institute (ITI) is a renowned institution that has been providing technical education since 1931. Originally known as the St. Theresa’s Industrial School, it was established with the aim of providing vocational training to the children of textile mill workers who had migrated to Salem from surrounding villages. During this period, there was a significant shift towards urban migration as agricultural activities suffered due to years of poor monsoon.

Over the years, the St. Theresa’s Industrial School has evolved to become a leading ITI in the state of Tamil Nadu, offering National Council of Vocational Training (NCVT) Certificate courses in all four trades. The institution has been approved by the State Council of Vocational Training (SCVT), reflecting its commitment to quality education and excellence.

Mechanic Motor Vehicle

Mechanic Motor Vehicle is a two-year certificate course offered by St Theresa's Industrial Training Institute, Salem, aimed at providing technical training in the repair and maintenance of motor vehicles. The course is affiliated with the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT) and provides hands-on training to students in diagnosing and fixing vehicle problems, engine tuning, brake repair, and other related skills

Turner

The Turner course at St Theresa's ITI is a two-year program that trains students in precision engineering techniques. The course covers topics such as turning operations, precision measurement, machine maintenance, and safety practices. Students gain practical experience in the operation of lathes, milling machines, grinders, and other precision tools. Graduates of the program are equipped with the skills necessary to pursue careers in the manufacturing industry as machinists, tool and die makers, or CNC operators.
